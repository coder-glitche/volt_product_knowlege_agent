# Selfie capture journey

In the Tata journey, we have a step to capture a selfie, but this is not included in the Bajaj journey.

While the selfie feature is part of the Bajaj Figma design, it is neither implemented in production nor required.

**User Flow for Selfie Capture (Bajaj Journey):**

1. The user sees a "Click Selfie" button, which activates the front camera after obtaining permission.
2. If an MFD creates the application, they can share a link with the customer.
3. The customer flow is as follows:
    - MFD shares the link with the customer.
    - Customer receives the link and opens it.
    - Customer logs in by verifying their phone number and entering the OTP.
    - The customer continues the application, completes KYC, and provides camera permissions.
    - Customer clicks the "Click Selfie" button, captures, and uploads the selfie.
    - Once the selfie is verified, the customer proceeds to the next steps.
