# Re-engagement Campaign Message Templates

1. **Segment Definition:**
- Create 3 segments based on time since empanelment: [https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1G_4aPZn5m2YpGtMWaAKz5kGLTVihWlO0Ls7XnhO9XYs/edit?usp=sharing](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1G_4aPZn5m2YpGtMWaAKz5kGLTVihWlO0Ls7XnhO9XYs/edit?usp=sharing)

    - Mid-term (31-90 days): 1,244 partners
    - Long-term (90+ days): 9,763 partners
1. **Experiment Design:**
- Split each segment into 3 groups:
    - Control Group (20%)
    - Treatment Group A (40%): Personalized WhatsApp/SMS
    - Treatment Group B (40%): WhatsApp/SMS + Email follow-up
1. **Intervention Plan:**
- Treatment A:
    - Day 1: Initial WhatsApp message with personalized activation link
    - Day 3: SMS reminder with key benefits
    - Day 7: Final WhatsApp message with time-limited incentive
- Treatment B:
    - Day 1: WhatsApp message + Email with detailed activation guide
    - Day 3: SMS reminder + Email success stories
    - Day 7: Final WhatsApp + Email with time-limited incentive

## Design Guidelines

1. All communications should:
    - Focus on solving client redemption problems
    - Emphasize speed (5-minute processing)
    - Highlight the win-win (client retains investments, you earn commission)
    - Include social proof (2000+ partners)
2. A/B Testing Variables:
    - Benefit emphasis (speed vs convenience vs growth)
    - Commission incentive placement
    - Success story inclusion
    - CTA variations
3. Technical Tracking:
    - UTM parameters for all links
    - Segment-specific tracking codes
    - Response rate monitoring
    - Conversion tracking (successful client referrals)
