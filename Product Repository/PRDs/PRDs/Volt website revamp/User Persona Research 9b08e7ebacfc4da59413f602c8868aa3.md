### Established Investor

# Meet Rajesh Gupta - The Established Business Owner

## Demographics

- **Age:** 44 years old
- **Location:** Tier 1 city (Mumbai/Delhi/Bangalore)
- **Profession:** Business Owner
- **Income Level:** Upper middle class to affluent
- **Family Status:** Married with family responsibilities

## Financial Profile

- **Loan Requirements:** ₹7.35L (average for business customers)
- **Primary Loan Purpose:** Working capital for business
- **Investment Portfolio:** Active mutual fund investor
- **Existing Loans:** Has a car loan and possibly a home loan
- **Financial Behavior:** Financially aware but prefers quick solutions

## Digital Behavior

- **Social Media Usage:** Moderate user of Facebook and Linkedin
- **Content Consumption:** Actively watches financial/business content (56%)
- **Preferred Platforms:** Newsletters (31%), Facebook (28%), YouTube (21%)
- **Financial Influencers:** Follows personalities like Rachna Ranade, Akshat Srivastava

## Pain Points & Needs

- **Time Sensitivity:** Needs immediate access to funds (74% unplanned loans)
- **Process Friction:** Avoids traditional banks due to lengthy processes (69% don't approach banks first)
- **Alternative Consideration:** Considers personal loans (41%) or borrowing (23%) before LAMF
- **Social Constraints:** Hesitant to borrow from personal network despite considering it

## Decision Making Behavior

- **Planning:** Generally makes quick, unplanned financial decisions for business needs
- **Research:** Limited research into alternatives due to urgency
- **Priority:** Views liquidating mutual funds as last resort but values speed and convenience
- **Motivation:** Driven by immediate business requirements rather than personal expenses

## Product Expectations

- **Core Needs:** Quick disbursement, minimal documentation
- **User Experience:** Expects digital-first, seamless experience
- **Post-Service:** Wants clear visibility of loan status, EMIs, and interest calculations
- **Communication:** Prefers digital updates but appreciates timely reminders for payments

## Quote

"Quick process, bethe bethe kaam ho gaya" (The work was done while sitting in one place)

- **Location:** Tier 1 city (Bangalore/Mumbai)

- **Income:** ₹18-25 LPA
- **Living Situation:** Single, lives independently in a rented apartment

- **Loan Requirements:** ₹3-6L range

- **Existing Financial Products:** Credit card, personal loan app (just in case), UPI

- **Primary Device:** Smartphone for all financial activities

- **Content Consumption:**

    - Twitter finance community

- **Learning Style:** Video content, bite-sized information, community discussions

- **Investment Style:**

- **Knowledge:**

## Goals & Aspirations

- Short-term:

    - Travel plans (Europe trip)
    - Buying a new car
- Long-term:
    - Financial independence by 40
    - Starting own venture
    - Real estate investment

## Decision Making Process

- **Research:**
    - Compares multiple options digitally
    - Reads user reviews and ratings
    - Consults online communities
    - Uses comparison websites
- **Influences:**
    - Peer experiences and recommendations
    - Social media financial influencers
    - App ratings and user reviews

## Preferred Product Features

- **Must-haves:**
    - Fully digital process
    - Transparent pricing
    - Quick approval/disbursement
    - Modern, intuitive app interface
    - Clear dashboard with all information
    - Easy to consume content
- **Nice-to-haves:**
    - Rewards/cashback

    - 24/7 customer support through chat

- **Location:** Tier 2/3 city (Indore/Nagpur/Coimbatore)
- **Profession:** Wholesale Trading Business (Family Business)
- **Income:** ₹8-12 LPA (variable based on business)

    - Traditional investment mindset
    - 40% Fixed Deposits
    - 30% Property

    - 10% Gold
- **Loan Requirements:** ₹5-10L range
- **Primary Loan Purpose:** Business expansion, inventory purchase, children's education
- **Existing Loans:** Home loan with PSU bank

- **Primary Device:** Basic smartphone usage
- **Apps Used:**
    - WhatsApp for business
    - PhonePe for basic transactions
    - Bank's mobile app (basic features only)
- **Digital Challenges:**
    - Prefers face-to-face interactions for financial decisions
    - Limited comfort with English interfaces
    - Skeptical of purely digital processes
    - Relies on children/younger staff for complex digital tasks

## Financial Behavior & Trust Factors

- **Banking Relationships:**
    - Long-term relationship with local PSU bank branch
    - Knows bank manager personally
    - Values relationship-based banking
- **Decision Making:**
    - Relies heavily on chartered accountant's advice
    - Consults family members for major decisions
    - Prefers well-known institutions
    - Values personal references

## Pain Points & Fears

- **Documentation:**
    - Struggles with extensive paperwork
    - Difficulty in providing formal business documents
    - Limited digital documentation
- **Process Related:**
    - Uncomfortable with English-only interfaces
    - Hesitant about online-only processes
    - Needs in-person verification for trust
- **Trust Issues:**
    - Worried about digital fraud
    - Concerned about data privacy
    - Skeptical of new financial institutions
    - Fears hidden charges

## Goals & Aspirations

- Short-term:
    - Expand business inventory
    - Children's education planning
    - Marriage savings for daughter
- Long-term:
    - Business expansion to nearby cities
    - Securing children's future
    - Retirement planning through property

## Support Needs

- **Language:**
    - Vernacular support crucial
    - Multilingual customer service
- **Process:**
    - Hybrid model (digital + physical presence)
    - Local representative for trust-building
    - Step-by-step guidance
- **Documentation:**
    - Assistance with paperwork
    - Flexible documentation requirements
    - Simple verification process

## Preferred Communication Channels

- **Primary:**
    - WhatsApp updates
    - Phone calls
    - Local representative visits
- **Secondary:**
    - SMS alerts
    - Mobile app (if in local language)
- **Least Preferred:**
    - Email
    - Web portal

## Quote

"Main traditional bank se loan leta hun kyunki wahan mere pehchan ke log hai. Naye system me vishwas karne me time lagta hai."

## Questionnaire: User Calls [20]

### Warm-Up Questions

1. How did you hear about Volt money?
2. How has your experience been using volt?
3. What do you do, must have been a long day?
4. When and how did you start investing in Mutual Funds?

### **Exploring Financial Interests and Habitsw**

1. When and how did you start investing in Mutual Funds?
    - "Walk me through how you chose that particular fund?"
    - "What other investment options did you consider at that time?
2. How often do you plan your finances?
    1. Do you do it every year, month or daily?
    2. What tool do you use to track the same
3. Tell me about the last unexpected expense/financial emergency you faced?
    - "Walk me through exactly what you did to handle it?"
    - "What options did you consider?"
    - "How did you decide which option to go with?"
4. When was the last time you used an asset (like gold, FD, shares) i.e break our FD, Shares or MF?
5. Which banks do you currently have relationships with, and how would you rate your experience with them? 
    1. Why did you rate your experience with XYZ more than the rest?

### Identifying Influences

1. Are there any stories, experiences, or advice from others that have shaped how you approach loans or financial planning?
    1. Are there any friends, family members, or experts whose financial advice you trust implicitly? Why?
2. What sources do you rely on for financial research on your investments, liquidity and overall planning?
3. What financial apps do you like the most and why?

### Uncovering Motivations and Goals

- Imagine you’re looking at two different lenders. What kind of things make you feel one is better than the other?
- What's the next big purchase or investment you're planning?
- What are the various needs or reasons you take loan/credit for?
    - How urgently did you need the loan?
    - How long were you willing to take the loan for?
- What’s a goal or dream you’ve been working toward for a while? How important is it to you?
    - (Esa koi sapna hai jiske liye aap paise ikhate kar rhe ho)
- Do you hold any preference between asset backed loans and unsecured loans? If so why?

### Addressing Pain Points and Frustrations

- If you’ve applied for a loan before, what part of the process felt most stressful or frustrating?
- What’s your biggest concern when considering a loan, and how does it influence your decision-making?
- Have you ever felt let down by a financial institution or product? What happened, and how did it impact you?
- What’s your biggest concern when considering a secured loan?

## Questionnaire: Sales [5]

### Sales segmentation of customers

- Is there a particular grouping you guys do to segment the customers?
- Can you share an example of a sales call which helped you understand our customers better?

### Pain points

- What’s the most frequent question or concern customers have when they approach you?
- What are some common reasons customers express interest in loans against financial assets?
- Do customers typically come to you with a specific need or goal, or are they exploring options?

### **Identifying Influences**

1. Do customers mention being influenced by any specific people or platforms when deciding to take a loan (e.g., financial advisors, family, social media)?
2. How do customers usually find out about your services—through referrals, advertisements, or direct research?
3. Are there particular questions customers ask that indicate they’ve already done some research?
4. Do customers often compare your offerings with other financial institutions? What comparisons stand out?
5. Have you noticed any trends in the type of information customers seek before committing to a loan?

### Understanding Motivations and Goals

- How do customers frame their motivation—are they solving a problem, seizing an opportunity, or meeting a long-term goal?

### Customer Pain Points and Frustrations

- Do customers express concerns about the safety of their pledged assets? How do they describe these concerns?
- Are there misconceptions customers often have about loans against assets, and how do you address them?
1. What’s one feature or benefit customers frequently wish was part of your service?

### Needs and Expectations

- What do customers emphasize as their top priority when choosing a loan (e.g., speed, interest rates, transparency)?
- Are there specific features or services customers seem to value most (e.g., flexible repayment, digital processes)?
