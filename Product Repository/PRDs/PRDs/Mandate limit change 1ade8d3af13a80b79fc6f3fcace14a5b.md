## Key Insights:

1. Three-quarters of all applications (75.07%) receive modest credit limit increases below 100%.
2. The vast majority of applications (94.82%) have credit limit increases below 400%.
3. Applications with increases between 400% and 1000% represent only 3.66% of the dataset.
4. Very large increases (over 1000%) account for just 1.52% of all applications.
5. The 10K-25K range has the highest proportion of 400%+ increases (24.68%), while the 25L+ range has the lowest (1.12%).
6. As initial credit limits increase, the percentage of applications receiving 400%+ increases tends to decrease significantly, showing an inverse relationship between initial limit size and percentage increase.
