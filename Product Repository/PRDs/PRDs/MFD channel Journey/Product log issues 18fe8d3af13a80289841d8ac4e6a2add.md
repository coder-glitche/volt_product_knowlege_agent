## Summary Statistics

- Total Unique Issues: ~80+
- Most Frequent: Partner Portal 400/403 errors (15+ instances)
- Highest Impact: DigiLocker outage (78+ customers affected)
- Longest Duration Issue: TATA Agreement (5 days)

## Key Observations

1. Partner portal access and verification issues form the majority of reported problems
2. System integration issues (DigiLocker, RTA) caused the widest impact
3. Communication and display issues created significant confusion for partners
4. Payment and transaction issues, while fewer, had high business impact
