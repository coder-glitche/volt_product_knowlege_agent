**MFD Servicing Overview**

To ensure effective MFD servicing, we need to:

- **Provide quick acknowledgment** of messages or calls from MFDs.
- After acknowledgment, establish a **reliable TAT (Turnaround Time)** for ticket resolution.
- Progressively improve metrics to deliver a **responsive and helpful servicing experience**.

**Current Problems:**

1. **Lack of visibility** on RM responsiveness to WA messages, resulting in escalations due to non-responses.
2. **Lack of tracking** for ticket disposition and TAT beyond Ops and Tech tickets.

**Goal:**

Create a servicing setup that ensures:

- Responses within a defined **SLA (Service Level Agreement)**.
- Acknowledgment and clear TAT updates for MFD tickets.

**Requirements:**

1. **WA platform** with robust inbuilt chat analytics.
2. **Comprehensive ticketing system** for RMs to manage issues, MFD details, and ticket dispositions.

**Current Tools and Gaps:**

- *Periskope*: Lacks the required analytics, though Wati fulfills this need.
- *Zendesk*: Effective as a ticketing system but doesn’t support ticket creation directly from chat.

**Recommendation:**

1. Transition from Periskope to Wati for most MFDs, except top-tier ones.
    - Wati offers superior chat response metrics and analytics.
    - Its chatbot functionality enhances responsiveness and simplifies ticket resolution for basic queries.
2. Expand Zendesk usage to include tickets for non-Ops/Tech issues.

**Next Steps:**

1. Identify current **response TAT** and **resolution TAT**.
2. Expand ticket coverage to include more types of requests and issues.
3. Explore a more integrated solution for ticketing directly from chat.
