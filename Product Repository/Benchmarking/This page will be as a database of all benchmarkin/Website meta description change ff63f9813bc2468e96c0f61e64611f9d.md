### Learnings

1. Ideal length of title of search is 60-65 characters for better clicks and avoid Google rewriting the title.
2. Ideal length of meta text 150-155 characters more than that is truncated by the Google.
3. We can not specify what part of the meta details will be shown in bold. Common words from the search are automatically shown as bold.
