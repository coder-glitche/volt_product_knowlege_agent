# Research

Volt’s Design Fundamental Pillars

1. Simplicity
2. Speed
3. Transparency
4. Helpful

**Fundamentals of UX Writing (for fintech apps)**

At its core, UX writing is about:

✅ **Guiding** users through tasks: What to do

✅ Making **complex information simple**: Information clarity

✅ **Building trust** and confidence: Reassurance 

✅ Ensuring **accessibility**: Less jargon more simple words
